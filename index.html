Safety Fundamentals

Mission

Safety Fundamentals was created to teach people some basics of emergency response. Instead of being just a bystander that doesn't know what to do, you can help with the knowledge you learn on this website.

*Disclaimer: This does NOT replace in-person training and certifications.*

Emergency Action Steps

Follow These Steps:

  1. Stay calm.
  2. Check the scene for safety.
  3. Check the victim.
  4. Call 9-1-1.
  5. Care for the victim.

When calling 9-1-1 the operator will need to know:

  1. Exact address or location with cross streets, landmarks, and building, floor, or room number.
  2. Your name and the telephone number you are calling from.
  3. What happened - the nature of the emergency.
  4. Number of victims and types of injuries.
  5. The care provided.
